Ait El Hadj Hassan Matai

IAE St Nazair - KB London University - UC Louvain- HARVARD- ESSEC

Ait El Hadj Hassan Matai is a highly accomplished individual with a strong academic background. He holds degrees from IAE St Nazair, KB London University, UC Louvain, HARVARD, and ESSEC, highlighting his academic excellence.
As the PDG Fondateur of Across Academy, Ait El Hadj Hassan Matai is a visionary leader and entrepreneur.
He is also the President of the African Investors Council, demonstrating his commitment to promoting economic development and investment in Africa. Ait El Hadj Hassan Matai is a Corporate Governance and Business Mentor, and his expertise in this area has helped many businesses and organizations to achieve success.
He is also a Profiler, Écrivain chercheur en psychologie, with a deep understanding of human behavior and psychology. This knowledge has been invaluable in his work as a business mentor and leader. Moreover, Ait El Hadj Hassan Matai is an accomplished writer and researcher in the field of psychology. His insights and expertise have been instrumental in understanding human behavior, and he has contributed significantly to the development of this field.
Overall, Ait El Hadj Hassan Matai's diverse background and expertise in areas such as entrepreneurship, corporate governance, psychology, and business mentoring make him an invaluable asset to any organization or initiative he is associated with.
