Abdelhadi EL Falaki PhD

Université de Technologie de
Compiègne (UTC) - France

MAbdelhadi EL FALAKI PhD is a highly accomplished professional with expertise in the field of Biomedical Sciences and Engineering. He is currently associated with the Université de Technologie de Compiègne (UTC) in France, indicating his strong academic background.
As an Area Sales Professional at Siemens Healthineers, Abdelhadi is involved in selling healthcare technologies and equipment. His extensive knowledge in the field of Biomedical Sciences and Engineering makes him an ideal candidate for this role. Moreover, Abdelhadi also holds the position of Vice-President of the Société Marocaine Biomédicale, an organization inspired by biomedical sciences and engineering.
His leadership in this position reflects his passion for driving advancements in the field of healthcare and improving patient outcomes. Abdelhadi's expertise extends to the areas of healthcare territories and technologies, and he brings valuable insights to the table through his work. His dedication to improving the field of Biomedical Sciences and Engineering makes him an asset to any organization or initiative he is associated with.
