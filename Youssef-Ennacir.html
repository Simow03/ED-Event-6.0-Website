Youssef Ennacir

Ecole Mohammedia D'Ingenieurs - ISCAE

Youssef ENNACIRI is a highly accomplished individual with expertise in multiple fields. He is associated with the Ecole Mohammadia d'Ingénieurs / ISCAE, highlighting his strong academic background.
As the Directeur général of Emie Environment System, Youssef brings his engineering and entrepreneurial expertise to the table. He is also an expert in entrepreneurship and a consultant, indicating his extensive experience in the field of business and entrepreneurship. Furthermore, as a chroniqueur on television and radio, he is able to share his insights and knowledge with a wider audience. Youssef is passionate about several topics, including entrepreneurship, employability of young people, territorial governance, economic inclusion of youth, start-ups, and ToE (Time of Entrepreneurship). His work in these areas has contributed significantly to improving the lives of young people in his community.
Additionally, Youssef is an advocate for auto-entrepreneurship, and he has been instrumental in promoting and supporting the growth of small businesses and start-ups in his community.
Overall, Youssef ENNACIRI's diverse background and expertise in multiple fields, including engineering, entrepreneurship, and governance, make him an invaluable asset to any organization or initiative he is associated with.
